# Math & Chemistry Test Equations

## Basic Algebra

### Quadratic Formula
The solutions to \(ax^2 + bx + c = 0\) are given by:
\[x = \dfrac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}\]

### Vertex Form
A quadratic in vertex form: \(y = a(x - h)^2 + k\) where \((h, k)\) is the vertex.

### Distance Formula
The distance between two points:
\[d = \sqrt{(x_2 - x_1)^2 + (y_2 - y_1)^2}\]

## Trigonometry

### Basic Ratios
In a right triangle:
- \(\sin\theta = \dfrac{\text{opposite}}{\text{hypotenuse}}\)
- \(\cos\theta = \dfrac{\text{adjacent}}{\text{hypotenuse}}\)
- \(\tan\theta = \dfrac{\text{opposite}}{\text{adjacent}} = \dfrac{\sin\theta}{\cos\theta}\)

### Pythagorean Identity
\[\sin^2\theta + \cos^2\theta = 1\]

### Law of Cosines
\[c^2 = a^2 + b^2 - 2ab\cos\gamma\]

---

## Calculus

### Derivative Definition
The derivative as a limit:
\[f'(x) = \lim_{h \to 0} \dfrac{f(x + h) - f(x)}{h}\]

### Power Rule
\[\dfrac{d}{dx} x^n = nx^{n-1}\]

### Chain Rule
\[\dfrac{dy}{dx} = \dfrac{dy}{du} \cdot \dfrac{du}{dx}\]

### Integration
\[\int_a^b f(x)\,dx = F(b) - F(a)\]

---

## Vectors

### Magnitude
For \(\vec{v} = \langle a, b, c \rangle\):
\[\|\vec{v}\| = \sqrt{a^2 + b^2 + c^2}\]

### Dot Product
\[\vec{a} \cdot \vec{b} = a_xb_x + a_yb_y + a_zb_z = \|\vec{a}\|\|\vec{b}\|\cos\theta\]

### Cross Product
\[\vec{a} \times \vec{b} = \begin{vmatrix} \hat{i} & \hat{j} & \hat{k} \\ a_1 & a_2 & a_3 \\ b_1 & b_2 & b_3 \end{vmatrix}\]

## Chemistry

### Stoichiometry
Moles: \(n = \dfrac{m}{M}\) where \(m\) is mass and \(M\) is molar mass.

Molarity: \(c = \dfrac{n}{V}\) (mol/L)

Dilution equation:
\[c_1V_1 = c_2V_2\]

### Ideal Gas Law
\[PV = nRT\]

### Combined Gas Law
\[\dfrac{P_1V_1}{T_1} = \dfrac{P_2V_2}{T_2}\]

### Thermodynamics
Heat energy: \(q = mc\Delta T\)

Enthalpy change:
\[\Delta H = \dfrac{q}{n}\]

### Gibbs Free Energy
\[\Delta G = \Delta H - T\Delta S\]

### Equilibrium
\[K = \dfrac{[\text{products}]^{\text{coeff}}}{[\text{reactants}]^{\text{coeff}}}\]

### pH and pOH
\[\text{pH} = -\log[H^+]\]
\[\text{pOH} = -\log[OH^-\]
\[\text{pH} + \text{pOH} = 14\]

### Electrochemistry
Cell potential:
\[E^{\circ}_{\text{cell}} = E^{\circ}_{\text{cathode}} - E^{\circ}_{\text{anode}}\]

Free energy relation:
\[\Delta G^{\circ} = -nFE^{\circ}_{\text{cell}}\]

## Physics

### Kinematics
\[v = v_0 + at\]
\[x = x_0 + v_0t + \dfrac{1}{2}at^2\]
\[v^2 = v_0^2 + 2a(x - x_0)\]

### Forces
Newton's Second Law:
\[\vec{F} = m\vec{a}\]

Gravitational force:
\[F_g = G\dfrac{m_1m_2}{r^2}\]

### Energy
Kinetic energy: \(K = \dfrac{1}{2}mv^2\)

Potential energy: \(U = mgh\)

Total mechanical energy:
\[E = K + U = \dfrac{1}{2}mv^2 + mgh\]

### Einstein's Equation
\[E = mc^2\]

### Wave Equation
\[v = f\lambda\]

### Coulomb's Law
\[F = k\dfrac{q_1q_2}{r^2}\]
